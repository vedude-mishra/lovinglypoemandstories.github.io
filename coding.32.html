My Family And Their Love


Mummy mummy sometimes slaps on my cheeks,
I felt very ashamed,
But when once i silently walked in kitchen and saw you crying in alone,
You noticed me and hide your tears,
I never realized why you were crying .

Daddy daddy sometimes i request to buy my favorite toy,
And without hesitating you bought it to me,
But when i saw your room door was little open,
You were seeing your broken golf kit and were in tears,
I never realized why you were crying.

Sister sister sometimes i fight with you for a lollipop,
In Spite it was yours i always try to get it,
By seeing my hardship of getting it ,you purposely drop it and allow me to get it,
But when i peep into your room i saw you with a certificate in your hand and saw you in tears,
I never realized why you were crying.

But when i grew up i understood that you all care for me,
And allow me to be happy by giving up your happiness,
And now the same is happening with me ,
I always thought that why you were crying but ,
Now i understood that my family always cares for me.
